The Ancient Egyptians were pioneers in the development of one of the earliest and most influential calendars in human history. Living in a region dominated by the annual flooding of the Nile River, the Egyptians recognized the critical importance of accurately tracking the passage of time and the seasonal changes. Mathematical papyrus "Egypt geometry and astronomy".
Written evidence of the use of mathematics dates back to at least 3000 BC with the ivory labels found at Tomb Uj at Abydos.
